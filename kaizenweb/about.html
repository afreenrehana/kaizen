Who We Are

Pioneers of thermoset plastic moulding

Kaizen Technologies is a thermo set moulding company that manufactures thermo sets for cookware and electrical,automobile components

We have been manufacturing products of thermoset plastic since 20 years and Since thermoset mouldings are getting vanished our company manufactures thermoset components which are highly rigid compared to other thermoplastics electrically and heat resistance.

Our History

2001 -

We started with producing car socket brushbox to KHMD

2004 -

We got mould cavity for cooker handles,started manufacturing cooker handles for Prestige group

2009 -

As we got the machines for producing,We even started manufacturing 750W motor housing.

2012 -

We started connections with more companies to enlarge our sales of productions and with that we started focusing on manufacturing higher count of the products

Our Focus

We aim for Producing high ratio of products with efficient time.

Our company mainly focuses on 3 moulding techniques:

  • Injection Moulding
  • Compression Moulding
  • Transfer Moulding
